Welcome to the ICHydro news feed! This is where we'll share updates about our water quality monitoring projects, sensor deployments, and research activities.

## What to Expect

You'll find posts about:

- **Water Quality Monitoring** - Updates on our SMARTWATER project and sensor networks
- **Sensor Deployments** - Field work and new installations across our monitoring sites
- **Research Updates** - Latest findings and collaborative projects
- **Technical Insights** - Deep dives into LoRaWAN, ThingsBoard, and our monitoring systems

## Stay Connected

Posts will be published directly from Slack using our `/publish-news` command, making it easy for the team to share updates in real-time.

Looking forward to sharing our journey with you!
